iCPSS Risk Score Calculator

Lanino L, Hunter AM, et al. Molecular-Based Ecosystem to Improve Personalized Medicine in Chronic Myelomonocytic Leukemia. In press.

iCPSS (International CMML Prognostic Scoring System) risk calculator for Chronic Myelomonocytic Leukemia. Single patient and cohort scoring.

Enter clinical and molecular data to calculate the iCPSS risk class.

Step 2 — Cytogenetic Risk
Classified according to the CPSS cytogenetic risk categories.
Low: normal karyotype or −Y
Intermediate: any other abnormality
High: complex karyotype (≥3 abnormalities), trisomy 8, or chr. 7 abnormalities
